Frequently Asked Questions

Is my payment secure?

Yes! We use PayPal's industry-leading encryption and security protocols. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods through PayPal, including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal balance.

Can I change my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime. Changes take effect immediately for upgrades or at the end of billing period for downgrades.

What if I need a refund?

Refund requests can be made within 30 days of purchase. Contact our support team for assistance.

How do I access my license key?

After payment, your license key is displayed immediately and also sent to your email address.

Do you offer API access?

Yes! Professional and Basic plan holders get full API access. See documentation for details.
